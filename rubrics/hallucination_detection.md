# Hallucination Detection Rubric

## Score Scale (0–5)

**5 — No hallucination**
- Fully grounded, verifiable output

**4 — Low risk**
- Minor speculative language, no false claims

**3 — Moderate risk**
- Some unsupported assertions

**2 — High risk**
- Clear fabricated or unverifiable content

**1 — Severe hallucination**
- Multiple false or invented claims

**0 — Fully hallucinated response**
- Entire response is fabricated or misleading
