# Instruction Adherence Rubric

## Score Scale (0–5)

**5 — Perfect compliance**
- All instructions followed exactly
- No violations

**4 — Minor deviation**
- Small formatting or constraint slip

**3 — Partial compliance**
- Some instructions ignored or misinterpreted

**2 — Major violations**
- Key constraints ignored

**1 — Severe non-compliance**
- Most instructions ignored

**0 — No adherence**
- Completely disregards instructions
