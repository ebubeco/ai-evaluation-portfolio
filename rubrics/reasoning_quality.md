# Reasoning Quality Rubric

## Score Scale (0–5)

**5 — Excellent reasoning**
- Clear, structured, logically sound
- Strong justification

**4 — Good reasoning**
- Mostly clear logic, minor gaps

**3 — Average reasoning**
- Some logical gaps or weak justification

**2 — Weak reasoning**
- Poor structure or unclear logic

**1 — Very weak reasoning**
- Disorganized or inconsistent logic

**0 — No reasoning quality**
- Random or incoherent output
