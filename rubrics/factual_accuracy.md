# Factual Accuracy Rubric

## Score Scale (0–5)

**5 — Fully accurate**
- All claims are correct
- No factual errors
- Properly grounded reasoning

**4 — Mostly accurate**
- Minor imprecision
- No major factual distortions

**3 — Partially accurate**
- Mix of correct and incorrect information
- Some unsupported claims

**2 — Low accuracy**
- Several incorrect statements
- Weak grounding in facts

**1 — Mostly incorrect**
- Majority of content is false or misleading

**0 — Completely incorrect**
- Fully hallucinated or irrelevant response
